-- 10) 查询当前总的可用资产收益(被冻结的资产除外)前三名的客户的名称、身份证号及其总收益，按收益降序输出，总收益命名为total_income。不考虑并列排名情形。
--    请用一条SQL语句实现该查询：
SELECT c_name,c_id_card,total_income 
FROM 
    client,
    (SELECT SUM(pro_income) AS total_income ,pro_c_id
    FROM property
    WHERE pro_status="可用"
    GROUP BY pro_c_id) c_income
WHERE c_id=pro_c_id
ORDER BY total_income DESC
LIMIT 0,3;
